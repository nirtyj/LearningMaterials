System Overview вҖ” 30,000ft Architecture

The entire infrastructure spans four deployment zones: Edge (on-robot), Fog (per-warehouse), Cloud (central platform), and GPU Cloud (training + simulation). Data flows from physical robots through real-time processing to an analytics lake, while models flow in the reverse direction вҖ” from training through validation to edge deployment.

End-to-End Data Flow

Data flows left-to-right from physical robots through three processing tiers, while models flow right-to-left from training back to edge deployment. The two flows meet at the Data Lake, which serves both as the sink for operational telemetry and the source for training datasets.
